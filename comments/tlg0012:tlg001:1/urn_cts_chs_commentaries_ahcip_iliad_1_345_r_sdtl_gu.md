<p>The name Patroklos=Patrokleēs means ‘he who has the glory [<em>kleos</em>] of the ancestors [<em>pateres</em>]’. On <em>kleos</em> in the sense of an overall reference to the ‘glory’ of poetry, see I.02.325.  </p>
